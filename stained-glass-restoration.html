Stained Glass Restoration

Church of the Nativity | Union, SC

Restored Stained Glass dramatically improves the appearance of buildings. Shenandoah Restorations, Inc. has the necessary tools, equipment, and suppliers to insure your stained glass restoration is completed in a timely manner. Restored stained glass is very close to the original condition, but with improvements that correct any imperfections. Your restored stained glass will last longer than when it was originally made.

We take great pride in preserving the integrity and historical prominence of every building from our past, so you can be assured that the accuracy and workmanship will be the highest quality.
